About Me

I grew up in El Dorado, California, as the oldest of six kids. Coming from an entrepreneur home, at the age of fifteen I started my first Etsy business, which gave me some experience interacting with clients and working with them to create one of a kind products.

I received my AA degree in Interdisciplinary Studies from Folsom Lake Community College in 2015, and from there started running Dr. Michael Uro's Laser Center, a branch of a podiatry office, in May of 2016-February 2021. In that roll I daily interacted with patients, booking appointments, taking payments, and working with various programs in order to complete paperwork for the office.

With more time on my hands during the pandemic, I started crafting a blog on sourdough bread in the summer of 2020. That continued to develop and I found that I enjoyed working with the Weebly and Wordpress programs.

In 2022 I decided to expand my knowledge of computer programing by taking some courses from an online developing school called Treehouse. Through the courses I took, I learned beginning JavaScript, HTML, and CSS. I am continuing to grow in those areas by taking more courses in order to expand my knowledge of programing, becoming a better developer.
